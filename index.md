# Privacy Policy — ADHD Food Scanner

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

ADHD Food Scanner ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication — we never see your plaintext password)

### Scan Data

When you scan a food product, we collect and store:

- Product barcode
- Product name
- Ingredient analysis results (verdict and detected trigger ingredients)
- Timestamp of the scan

Scan history is only stored for Premium subscribers. Free users' scan data is not retained.

### Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app, including:

- App crashes and errors (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- General usage patterns (via Firebase Analytics)
- No personally identifiable information is linked to analytics data

### Purchase Information

If you subscribe to Premium, purchase transactions are processed by Apple. We do not store your payment information. Subscription status is managed via RevenueCat.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Email address** — to authenticate your account and allow sign-in
- **Scan history** — to display your past scans within the app (Premium only)
- **Analytics** — to understand how the app is used and improve features
- **Crash data** — to identify and fix bugs

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, located in the United States. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

- **Firebase (Google)** — authentication and data storage: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **RevenueCat** — subscription management: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
- **Open Food Facts** — ingredient database (no personal data shared): https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — all scan history is visible within the app
- **Delete** your account and all associated data — available in the Profile tab under "Delete Account"
- **Export** — contact us to request a copy of your data

## Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the app:

1. Open the **Profile** tab
2. Tap **Delete Account**
3. Confirm deletion

This permanently removes your email, account credentials, and all scan history from our systems.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
